Completed
Stationery Donation Drive
📍 Bhai Kanhaiya Ashram, Sirsa 📅 30th May 2025

Thousands of children from economically disadvantaged backgrounds lack access to basic school supplies — a barrier to learning that is unacceptable in the world's fourth largest economy. Hands2Hold came together and donated 50 packets of stationery to Bhai Kanhaiya Ashram, Sirsa. Co-founder Hiral Garg, along with six dedicated volunteers, distributed these kits to children aged 5–16 from economically disadvantaged and specially-abled backgrounds.

"Fifty stationery kits may seem like a small number, but for fifty children, they represented fifty new opportunities to learn, create, and dream."
