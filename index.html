Our Work

Projects & Initiatives

Two completed drives, countless lives touched — every project is a step forward.

Completed
Stationery Donation Drive
📍 Bhai Kanhaiya Ashram, Sirsa 📅 30th May 2025

Thousands of children from economically disadvantaged backgrounds lack access to basic school supplies — a barrier to learning that is unacceptable in the world's fourth largest economy. Hands2Hold came together and donated 50 packets of stationery to Bhai Kanhaiya Ashram, Sirsa. Co-founder Hiral, along with six dedicated volunteers, distributed these kits to children aged 5–16 from economically disadvantaged and specially-abled backgrounds.

"Fifty stationery kits may seem like a small number, but for fifty children, they represented fifty new opportunities to learn, create, and dream."
Completed
Period Care Drive
📍 Slum Area, Sirsa 📅 12th June 2025

Menstrual hygiene remains a taboo and a barrier for millions of women and girls. Hands2Hold took action by distributing pads, diapers, and atta to families living in the slum areas of Sirsa. The drive was led by our co-founder Hiral andreached 100+ people and directly supported 22 families — providing not just essential supplies, but dignity and care to those who need it most.

"Breaking the silence, one family at a time — because every woman deserves to live with dignity."
